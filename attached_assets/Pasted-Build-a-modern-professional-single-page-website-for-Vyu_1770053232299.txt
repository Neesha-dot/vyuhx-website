Design Guidelines
11.1 Color Palette

Primary: Dark Blue (#1e3a5f, #2d4a6b)
Accent: Cyan (#4dd0e1, #00bcd4)
Achievement: Gold/Orange (#f59e0b, #ea580c)
Success/Active: Green (#10b981, #059669)
Background: White (#ffffff), Light Gray (#f5f7fa)
Text: Dark Gray (#333333), Medium Gray (#666666)

11.2 Typography

Headings: Bold, large scale (text-4xl to text-6xl)
Subheadings: Medium weight (text-xl to text-2xl)
Body: Regular weight (text-base to text-lg)
Timeline Dates: Small, uppercase, semibold
Font: Modern sans-serif (Inter, Poppins, or similar)

11.3 Spacing & Layout

Generous padding (py-16 to py-24 for sections)
Consistent margin between elements
Max-width container (max-w-7xl) for content
Border radius: rounded-xl for cards, rounded-full for buttons and technology pills
Timeline: 100px spacing between milestones (desktop), 60px (mobile)

11.4 Component Styles

Cards: White background, subtle shadow, hover lift effect
Buttons: Rounded (rounded-lg), padding (px-6 py-3), smooth transitions
Inputs: Border on focus, floating labels, proper spacing
Icons: Consistent size, proper spacing, gradient backgrounds
Technology Pills: Rounded-full, white/light background, icon + text layout
Timeline Cards: Extra padding, colored accent borders, icons with gradients
Badges: Small, rounded-full, colored backgrounds with white text

11.5 Responsive Breakpoints

Mobile: < 768px (single column, stacked layout, left-aligned timeline)
Tablet: 768px - 1024px (2-column grids)
Desktop: > 1024px (multi-column grids, side-by-side layouts, center timeline)
